Johnny Depp's Timeline

  • Jun 9, 1963 - Johnny Depp was born to John and Betty Sue Depp in Owenboro, Kentucky.
  • Jan 1, 1970 - Growing up the Depp family was constantly moving around. John Depp (his father) was a civil engineer and in order to accommodate to his job it was necessary that they move around frequently. Finally, when Johnny was 7 they settled in Miramar, Florida. The beginning of their new life in Florida was rough: "They lived in a motel for nearly a year, until his father found a job". Depp Hated his new home and became involved in trouble.
  • Jan 1, 1978 - When Johnny was 15, the split between his parents ultimately caused a rift between Johnny and his father and led him into more trouble. He claims that he hung out with the wrong crowd and broke into stores and the school just to cause trouble.
  • Jan 1, 1979 - At 16 years old, Johnny dropped out of school to become a musician and joined a band called "The Kids" and they were quite successful for a short amount of time.
  • Jan 1, 1984 - With the help of a friend Johnny was introduced into the world of acting where he thrived.
  • Jan 1, 1987 - Johnny grew a serious interest in acting and began to take lessons at the Loft Studio and eventually landed a position that jump started his career. Johnny took the role of Tommy Hanson in the hit show 21 Jump street.
  • Jan 1, 1998 - While filming the movie the "Ninth Gate", Depp met Vanessa Paradis, A French actress, singer and model and eventually they had two kids, Lily-Rose and Jack.
  • Jun 28, 2003 - Depp starts as Jack Sparrow in the movies "Pirates of the Caribbean" where he earned an Academy Award.
  • Jan 1, 2015 - While traveling to Australia Johnny came to issues with paperwork for his dogs can ran into legal trouble
  • Jan 1, 2016 - Johnny Depp becomes a box office star.
  • Dec 22, 2018 - Johnny Depp officially dropped from Pirates of the Caribbean.
